About 4KILO Campus Shop

We started our journey in 2025 with a simple mission: to help 4Kilo campus students get what they want as soon as possible, delivered right to their place.

Today, we're proud to serve hundreds of students with quality products, fair prices, and lightning-fast delivery. Your satisfaction is our success.

Frequently Asked Questions

❓ How fast is delivery?
We deliver within 30-45 minutes during working hours (12 PM - 3 PM). Orders placed outside these hours will be confirmed and delivered the next available time.
❓ Do you deliver to all blocks?
Yes! We deliver to all blocks in 4 Kilo campus for FREE. For 5 and 6 Kilo, it's 20 birr per delivery.
❓ Can I return products?
All products must be in original condition and unopened. Contact us within 24 hours of delivery if you have any issues.
❓ What payment methods do you accept?
We accept cash on delivery, bank transfers (CBE), and Telebirr. Choose the method that works best for you.
❓ Is there a minimum order?
No minimum order required! You can order as little or as much as you need.
❓ How do I place an order?
Simply message us on Telegram @z4k_shop with what you want, your location/dorm, and we'll confirm with a price and delivery time.
